About SchoolEdu

SchoolEdu is an initiative of the U.S. Department of Education designed to provide equitable access to high-quality educational resources for all American students. Our platform aligns with state educational standards and offers curriculum materials, interactive learning tools, and professional development resources for educators.

Through partnerships with educational institutions and technology providers, we ensure that our resources remain current, effective, and accessible to students regardless of their location or socioeconomic background.
